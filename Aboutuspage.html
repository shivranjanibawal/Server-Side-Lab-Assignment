About IET DAVV College

IET DAVV College, established in 1997, is a leading institution in the field of engineering and technology education. It is affiliated with Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV) in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India.

Our vision is to provide world-class education and build industry-ready professionals. The college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering, management, and computer science.
